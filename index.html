About

Mr. Clos:

Andrew Clos is a mystical being that traveled from the distant galaxy of GN-z11 in the Year of Our Lord 2015, to bestow his divine knowledge upon us mortal beings (It is also said that he came to Earth mostly for the mountain biking). In his mortal disguise, he projects himself as a science teacher, (specializing in chemistry), but is often seen using his godlike powers to assist and make fun of his students.

The Official ClosReplace Browser Extension

The ClosReplace browser extension is currently being developed!

Coming soon™ for Chrome and Firefox

Automatically bolds any instance of "Clos", "Mr. Clos", or "Andrew Clos" in any webpage. In addition, it can also replace some or all pictures in webpages with the visage of the great Mr. Clos. Has options for word replacement, so you can replace words you despise with beautiful words such as "Clos"
